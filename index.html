How We Pay


The way that we pay our workers is that when you buy a product from our website the money will go to the company and then when we have the money we will link you to the best designer for that job. When you are happy with your design we will then pay the designer that worked on the project 80% of the pay. So this way we can make sure that are employees do not scam or steal any money.
